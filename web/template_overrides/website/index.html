Welcome aboard...

After a little bit of a hiatus, the system has been wiped, and now even more areas, better performance, and some new game mechanics are on the way!

DrasticalMUD is running Evennia with a bunch of nostalgic MUD areas imported from legacy MUDs. The thought is there will be an emphasis on exploration and farming/crafting mechanics. Not to say there won't be some hack-and-slash, too. MU*s and BBSes served a an amorphous purpose to different people: Social interaction, self-expression, hack-and-slash compulsion, turn-based strategy scheming, a weird artistic outlet... what else? It's clay in the shape of a virtual world.

There are currently a few puzzles, a bunch of socials, world exploration stats, and more as development progresses. Hopefully this will fully evolve out of a sandbox into something unique that people can enjoy.

Check out the list of areas that have been imported!

We are listed on and participate in Grapevine listings and chat.
